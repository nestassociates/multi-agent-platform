# Agent Onboarding Guide

Welcome to Nest Associates! This guide will help you complete your profile and get your personal real estate website live.

## Getting Started

After your account has been created by our admin team, you'll receive a welcome email with:
- Login link to the dashboard
- Your temporary password
- Instructions to get started

### First Login

1. Click the login link in your welcome email
2. Enter your email and temporary password
3. You'll be prompted to create a new secure password
4. Once logged in, you'll see your dashboard

## Completing Your Profile

Your personal website won't go live until you complete all required profile information. Here's what you need to do:

### Required Fields

To activate your site, you must complete these fields:

| Field | Description | Tips |
|-------|-------------|------|
| **Profile Photo** | Professional headshot | Use a high-quality, professional photo |
| **Bio** | About you section | 2-3 paragraphs about your experience and approach |
| **Phone Number** | Contact number | Include area code |
| **Office Address** | Your office location | Full address with postcode |
| **Commission Rates** | Your fee structure | Sales and lettings rates |

### Optional Fields

These are recommended but not required:

- Website URL
- LinkedIn profile
- Facebook page
- Twitter/X handle
- Instagram profile

### Profile Completion Progress

You'll see a progress bar showing your completion status:

- **0-50%**: Just started - missing key information
- **50-99%**: Making progress - some fields remaining
- **100%**: Complete - ready for admin review!

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Upload Your Photo

1. Go to **Profile** in the dashboard
2. Click on the photo placeholder
3. Select a professional headshot (JPG or PNG)
4. Crop and adjust as needed
5. Click **Save**

**Photo Tips**:
- Use a professional headshot (not a selfie)
- Good lighting, clean background
- Dress professionally
- Smile and look approachable
- Minimum 400x400 pixels

### Step 2: Write Your Bio

1. Scroll to the **Bio** section
2. Write 2-3 paragraphs about yourself including:
   - Your experience in real estate
   - Your area of expertise
   - Why clients should work with you
   - Personal touch (hobbies, community involvement)
3. Click **Save**

**Bio Tips**:
- Keep it personal but professional
- Mention your local expertise
- Include any certifications or awards
- Make it about how you help clients

**Example Bio**:
> With over 10 years of experience in the London property market, I specialise in helping families find their perfect home in South London. My deep knowledge of areas like Dulwich, Peckham, and Brixton means I can guide you to neighbourhoods that match your lifestyle.
>
> I believe buying or selling a home should be exciting, not stressful. That's why I'm committed to clear communication, honest advice, and being available when you need me.
>
> Outside of work, you'll find me exploring local parks with my family or volunteering at the community centre. I love this area and can't wait to help you discover why it's such a great place to live.

### Step 3: Add Contact Details

1. Enter your **Phone Number** (include area code)
2. Enter your **Office Address** (full address with postcode)
3. Click **Save**

### Step 4: Set Your Commission Rates

1. Go to the **Fees** section
2. Enter your **Sales Commission** rate (e.g., 1.25%)
3. Enter your **Lettings Commission** rate (e.g., 10% of annual rent)
4. Add any additional fee information
5. Click **Save**

### Step 5: Add Social Links (Optional)

1. Scroll to **Social Media** section
2. Add links to your professional profiles:
   - LinkedIn
   - Facebook
   - Twitter/X
   - Instagram
3. Click **Save**

## What Happens Next

### After You Complete Your Profile

1. **Automatic Review**: When you hit 100% completion, your profile is automatically sent for admin review
2. **Admin Approval**: Our team reviews your information (usually within 24 hours)
3. **Site Deployment**: Once approved, your personal website is built and deployed
4. **Activation Email**: You'll receive an email with your live site URL

### Your Personal Website

Your site will be live at:
```
https://your-name.nestassociates.co.uk
```

It will feature:
- Your profile and bio
- Your property listings (synced automatically from Apex27)
- Your contact information
- Your coverage areas
- Your reviews and testimonials

## Managing Your Site Content

Once your site is active, you can create and manage content:

### Creating Area Guides

1. Go to **Content** → **New**
2. Select **Area Guide**
3. Write about a local area you cover
4. Add photos and key information
5. Submit for review

### Creating Blog Posts

1. Go to **Content** → **New**
2. Select **Blog Post**
3. Write about market updates, tips, or local news
4. Submit for review

### Content Approval

All content goes through a quick review:
- **Pending**: Submitted, awaiting review
- **Approved**: Published on your site
- **Rejected**: Needs changes (you'll receive feedback)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long until my site is live?

Once you complete your profile:
- Admin review: 1-2 business days
- Site deployment: 30-60 minutes after approval

### Can I change my subdomain?

Contact the admin team to request a subdomain change. This can only be done before your site goes live.

### How do I update my profile?

Simply edit any field in your dashboard and click Save. Changes are reflected immediately on your site.

### My properties aren't showing

Properties sync automatically from Apex27. If you don't see them:
1. Verify they're listed in Apex27
2. Check they're assigned to your branch
3. Wait 15 minutes for sync to complete
4. Contact admin if still missing

### I made a mistake in my bio

You can edit your bio anytime from the Profile page. Changes appear on your site within minutes.

### How do I see my live site?

Click the **"View Site"** button in your dashboard header, or visit your subdomain directly.
